IoT-Based Irrigation System

The Atmospheric Water Generator, also known as the AWG, will generate water as a result of the dehumidification process. The water collected will be stored in a tank and will be used by the IOT-based irrigation system for watering the plants. The IOT-based irrigation system will determine whether or not to water the crops based on the soil moisture sensor. All of the information will be saved in a cloud database and may be accessed using a mobile application. Soil moisture, temperature, and the amount of water generated by AWG are all included in this data. In addition, the client can operate the device through the mobile application.
